FAQ

Questions before joining.

Is this a guaranteed remote job?

No. This program does not guarantee a job, interview, income, or specific result. It gives you a stronger system, clearer positioning, direct feedback, and accountability. Your results depend on your background, effort, market conditions, consistency, and the roles you target.

Who is this best for?

It is best for 9–5 workers, career switchers, and professionals who want to pursue remote roles but need help with resume positioning, LinkedIn, outreach, tracking, and interview prep.

What if I do not know what remote role to target?

That is part of the program. The first step is choosing a realistic target role based on your current background, skills, and goals.

Is this live or self-paced?

The beta includes resources, templates, a private Discord channel, and weekly group coaching/accountability. You will have tasks to complete each week.

Why is this a beta?

This is the first small group version. The goal is to work closely with early members, improve the program, and collect feedback before launching the larger version.

Can I use this if I already have a resume?

Yes. The goal is not to start from scratch unless needed. The goal is to make your current resume clearer, more targeted, and more proof-driven for remote roles.
